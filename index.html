Data Privacy Notice
Please read before continuing

Hitlist Hatcher processes medical readiness data entirely within your browser. No information is ever uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server.

  • ✓MRRS data is read locally and never leaves your device
  • ✓No analytics, cookies, or third-party services are used
  • ✓Settings (not PHI) may be saved to browser local storage for convenience
  • ✓Closing or refreshing the tab permanently discards all loaded data

This tool is intended for use on authorized government or command-approved devices only. Users are responsible for ensuring the device, browser, and work environment comply with applicable DoD, HIPAA, and Privacy Act requirements. MRRS data contains PII — handle accordingly.
